Core tribology equations

Sommerfeld number: S = (μ·N/P)·(R/C)²
Bearing pressure: P = W / (L·D)
Film thickness: h(θ) = C(1 + ε·cosθ)
Eccentricity: ε = e/C = 1 − h₀/C
Half-Sommerfeld P(θ): ∝ εsinθ(2+εcosθ) / (2+ε²)(1+εcosθ)²
Hersey number: Hn = μ·N / P
Friction coeff: f = (f·R/C)·(C/R) from R-B tables
RUL model: RUL = RUL₀ · (H/H₀)^α
Viscosity-temp: μ(T) = μ₀·exp(β/(T+273))

Design validation checklist

— h₀/C ratio check
— Bearing pressure vs material limit
— L/D ratio (0.5–1.0 range)
— h₀ vs surface roughness (Ra=0.3 µm)
— Oil temperature limit (< 120 °C)
— Friction coefficient (< 0.01 HL)
— Misalignment & edge loading
